Cake Mania 6.0

10am - 6pm CAT
Tue, 15th April, 2023
Emerald Events Center Uyo, Akwa Ibom Nigeria 

About Event

Cakemania is the biggest cake event in Nigeria that incorporates a live cake decorating competition, baking competition, cake eating, exhibition by top bakers, awards, and all of this is mixed with all-round entertainment. Our vision is to promote, inspire, and empower young bakers in Akwa Ibom and the entire baking industry. Cakemania has had 5 consecutive seasons successfully and now is time for its 6th edition in May 2024. Venue: The biggest event center in Akwa-Ibom - Emerald Events Center. For tickets, sponsorship, inquiries, and participation, call/text on WhatsApp at +2348081115858 or email us. Website for more details. Follow them on Socials: Facebook and Instagram @cakemania6
